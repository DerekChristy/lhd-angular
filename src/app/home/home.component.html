About Us

Local Hack Day: Build is a 24-hour hackathon that aims to create and grow an innovation-centric mindset and bring ideas to life through a community effort supported by mentors from the community. Over the course of 24 hours, attendees will use hardware and software to build the ideas that they have submitted during the selection round, from website to robots to mobile apps and much more. This is an event for all skill levels, from beginners to the most veteran developers. Workshop attendees will apply their skills to solve problems that will help us BUILD THE FUTURE.
